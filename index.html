artist statement

my name is Stella Cai and I work with digital art programs like Clip Studio Art and Procreate. I find that folklores, fairy tales, myths and religion to be a key part in my art inspiration. I love fantasy creatures like unicorns, vampires, mermaids and fairies! I also draw inspiration from historical fashion such as the Rococo era in France and the hanfu of ancient China.
