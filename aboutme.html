about the artist

hello my name is Stella and I am an aspiring illustrator. I am currently an undergrad at SJSU. What I like to do in my free time is to read mystery novels! I particularly enjoy Agatha Christie's novels and I find them engrossing. I also like to travel with friends to try local food and flavors! In addition, I enjoy designing my own clothing and I am interested in creating my own clothing brand some day! My current goal is to find a steady career in the arts department and possibly open up a small boutique of my own as mentioned previously.
